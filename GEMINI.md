# CommonPlace

CommonPlace is a social platform where users give and receive feedback on their hobbies.

It gained hundreds of users in the first stage, now that database is cleaned and we are relaunching with a more sustainable marketing plan. There is good reason to believe it will scale to thousands of users, and potentially hit the user acquisition and retention threshold required for word of mouth to begin.

Users see a TikTok-style feed of posts, where they tap an “impression” (or type a custom one) in order to move to the next post. These “impressions” are a long list of potential adjectives to describe the content. Users earn a point for each impression they give, and it costs 3 points to post something of your own. Impressions show up in a user’s Inbox, and they can respond to it easily, creating a network effect on top of the feedback effect and UGC SEO effect.

Every business takes years to earn revenue, so that’s the plan for CommonPlace too. I will upsell users on creative applications in the future, as well as opening things like Shop, Ads, etc.
